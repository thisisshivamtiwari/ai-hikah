1. Home Page

Our mission is to provide inclusive, values-driven education and enrichment that empowers learners of all ages to thrive with purpose and confidence.

What We Do:

• Deliver high-quality home education rooted in Islamic values

• Support schools and organisations through expert consultancy

2. About Us
Our Story:
Al-Hikmah was founded with a vision to create a nurturing, faith-inspired learning environment where families and learners feel supported, seen, and empowered. We recognised the need for a community-rooted educational model that honours both academic and spiritual development.

Vision:
A world where every learner is guided by wisdom, uplifted by community, and empowered to lead with purpose.

Mission & Values:
Our mission is to inspire hearts, educate minds, and empower lives through holistic, inclusive, and values-based learning.

Our HIKMAH Values:

• H — Honesty: We act with integrity and sincerity in all we do.
• I — Ilm (Knowledge): We seek and share knowledge that uplifts and enlightens.
• K — Kindness: We nurture compassion, empathy, and care for others.
• M — Motivation: We encourage ambition, resilience, and self-belief.
• A — Accountability: We take responsibility and strive for excellence.
• H — Harmony: We build unity through respect, diversity, and collaboration.
Meet the Team:
Our team includes experienced educators, mentors, subject specialists, and community leaders who are passionate about nurturing the next generation. All staff are DBS-checked and trained in safeguarding and inclusive practice.

Organisational Structure:
Al-Hikmah operates as a community-focused organisation, reinvesting in programmes that benefit learners and families. Our leadership team works collaboratively with educators, parents, and partners to ensure quality and impact.

Partners & Funders:
We work with local authorities, charities, schools, and community organisations who share our commitment to educational equity and empowerment.

3. Al-Hikmah Academy (Home Education)
Our Approach:
We provide a structured, values-based home education programme that supports academic achievement, character development, and spiritual growth.

Curriculum Overview:
Our curriculum integrates core national subjects with Islamic studies, Qur’an, and Arabic. It is designed to be engaging, inclusive, and responsive to individual learning needs.

Learning Pathways:
We offer flexible pathways for learners aged 5–16, with personalised support, small group teaching, and regular assessments to track progress.

Parent Information:
We work in partnership with parents, offering clear guidance on admissions, fees, and expectations. Our team is always available to support families on their home education journey.

Student Life:
Beyond academics, students benefit from pastoral care, enrichment activities, and opportunities to develop leadership, creativity, and confidence.

Resources:
Parents and students have access to downloadable resources, term dates, and a dedicated homework portal.
